Safety & Privacy

Power Tracker for Schools is designed with student privacy and safety as the top priority:

  • No ad tracking or third-party scripts
  • All data stays in your browser unless you choose to export
  • No personal information required to function
  • Complies with school privacy requirements

Student FAQ

Will this slow my Chromebook?

No! Power Tracker is designed to be lightweight and won't affect your browsing experience or device performance.

Does it collect my name or personal info?

Absolutely not. The extension doesn't collect any personal information - it only tracks anonymous energy usage data that stays on your device.

How do I remove it?

You can easily remove the extension through Chrome's standard extension controls - just go to Settings â†’ Extensions and click Remove.

Teacher & IT FAQ

Installation Requirements

Installation is only available through the Chrome Web Store - no sideloading required. The extension follows all Chrome Web Store policies and security guidelines.

Privacy Compliance

No personally identifiable information (PII) is needed for the extension to function. All data processing happens locally on the student's device.
